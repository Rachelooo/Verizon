About

Verizon Communications was created on June 30, 2000 by Bell Atlantic Corp.
and GTE Corp., in one of the largest mergers in U.S. business history. GTE and Bell Atlantic evolved and grew
through decades of mergers, acquisitions and divestitures.

Today, Verizon is a global communications technology company
delivering the promise of the digital world to millions of customers every day.
